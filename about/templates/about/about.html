A Smarter Way to Manage Your Work

Welcome to CTrack, a simple but powerful tool designed to help you stay on top of your work schedule and goals.

Steps

Create an account
In the register page create an account by filling the Sign Up form.

Adjust your settings
Once logged in, modify your daily target, weekly hours and rostered days off in the Profile section.

Record absences
Log any absences in the absence page so your target is reduced accordingly. (Meetings, van maintenance, sickness, holidays...)

Track your performance
Submit every job completed for each day in the Tracker section. These jobs are saved and can be modified or deleted in the job history section.

The goal is to make work tracking straightforward — no more spreadsheets, no more guesswork. Just a clean view of your schedule, your targets, and your progress.
Whether you’re reviewing your performance or planning ahead, CTrack gives you the clarity you need to stay on target.
