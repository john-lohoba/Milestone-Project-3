About Us

Welcome to CTrack, a simple but powerful tool designed to help you stay on top of your work schedule and goals.

With CTrack, you can:

  • Track your daily progress: Log completed jobs and instantly see how many credits you’ve earned for the day.
  • Stay aligned with your targets: Each day comes with a clear, adjusted target that takes into account your scheduled hours and any recorded absences.
  • Respect your time off: Your rostered days off are built right into the system, so you only focus on the days that matter.
  • Understand your week at a glance: Visualize your targets, credits, and absences for the entire week, helping you plan better and stay motivated.
Our goal is to make work tracking straightforward — no more spreadsheets, no more guesswork. Just a clean view of your schedule, your targets, and your progress.
Whether you’re reviewing your performance or planning ahead, CTrack gives you the clarity you need to balance productivity with rest.
